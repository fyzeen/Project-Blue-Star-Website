Our Vision

Project Blue Star seeks to increase connection between students and seniors. We believe that building love and understanding through meaningful intergenerational interactions can end social isolation and loneliness.

Our Goal

As high school and university students, our goal is to engage with senior citizens by holding sessions in community venues. At these sessions, seniors and students enjoy time together listening/creating music and participating in various unstructured activities.

We emphasize meaningful conversation between students and seniors, a simple activity shown to alleviate feelings of loneliness.

Our Name

The Blue Star Lotus is a symbol of rebirth — a connection between young and old. Given our mission to connect students and seniors, it only fit to name our organization after this symbolic flower.

Who We Are

This organization was founded in 2021 by National Honor Society members and music students at Technical High School in St. Cloud, MN. We have since grown to include student volunteers from various organizations and schools throughout Minnesota.
