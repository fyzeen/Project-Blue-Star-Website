Our Vision

Project Blue Star seeks to improve the lives of seniors in Central Minnesota. This includes all aspects of a healthy life (physical, mental and emotional). We believe that our work can make a lasting impact on the most vulnerable members of our community.

Our Goal

As high school students, our goal is to engage with senior citizens by holding monthly sessions in senior facilities. At these sessions, residents have the opportunity to participate in mentally engaging activities alongside student volunteers.

We emphasize interaction and conversation between students and seniors, a simple activity shown to alleviate feelings of loneliness.

Our Name

The Blue Star Lotus is a symbol of rebirth — a connection between young and old. Given our mission to connect students and seniors, it only fit to name our organization after this symbolic flower.

Who We Are

This organization was founded by National Honor Society members at Technical High School in St. Cloud. The entire organization is based on the NHS values of service, volunteerism, and character.
