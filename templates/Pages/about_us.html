Our Vision

Project Blue Star seeks to improve the lives of seniors in Central Minnesota. This includes all aspects of a healthy life (physical, mental and emotional). We believe that our work can make a lasting impact on the most vulnerable members of our community.

Our Goal

Our goal as high school students running Project Blue Star is to interact with senior citizens by holding sessions in various senior homes where residents have the opportunity to participate in mentally engaging activities alongside student volunteers.

During these sessions, we emphasize interaction between students and seniors, who often become isolated and lonely in assisted living environments.

Our Name

The Blue Star Lotus, the national flower of Bangladesh, is a symbol of rebirth — a connection between young and old. Given our mission to connect students and seniors, it only fit to name our organization after this symbolic flower.

Who We Are

This organization was founded by and is run by National Honors Society members at Technical High School in St. Cloud. The entire organization is based on the NHS values of service, volunteerism, and character.
